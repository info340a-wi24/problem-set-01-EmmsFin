Percy Jackson

Percy Jackson is a demigod child of Sally Jackson and Posidon. He is the main character and sole narrator of Percy Jackson and the Olympian series by Rick Riordon. He is also the a main character and occasional narrator of the Heroes of Olympus series. In 2010 and 2013 movies based of the first two books in the series were released with Logan Lerman playing Percy Jackson. The movies were enteraining but entirely inaccurate, thus, similarly to the Avatar the Last Airbender movie, they "do not exist." In late 2023/early 2024, a Disney+ series called Percy Jackson and the Olympians was released, with 5 epidodes already released and 3 episodes left.

There are many aspects I admire about Percy Jackson, here are just a few:

  • Percy Jackson has dyslexia and ADHD. As a child struggling with dyslexia, reading the Percy Jackson series with a main character that faced the same learning difficulties as it was extremely influential in my learning to love reading!
  • Percy Jackson has strong morals and typically thinks about the greater good over his own needs. For example *spoiler alert* he is willing to sacrifice immortality to help other demigods receive recognition and parental-support from their godly parents.
  • Percy Jackson is able to eat, and is obsessed with, blue dye. As someone that is allergic to blue dye, this is an amazing feat.
